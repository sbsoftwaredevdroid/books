Camera Usage

« BibliBooks » application only uses the device camera on user’s request. In order to use the camera, a permission is needed. Starting at Android 6.0 Marshmallow, users can choose to decline this permission. Before Android 6.0 Marshmallow, users have to accept the permission before installing the application, according to Google and Android policies.

The camera is used in two different ways in « BibliBooks » :

    - Users can take a picture that will be referred as a book cover in the application.
    - Users can scan a barcode with the camera directly in the application. The scannedbarcode is then processed by the application for find information about the book scanned.

Data Collected

« BibliBooks » gathers anonymous usage data in order to improve the quality of the service.

The data is processed within Firebase and allows the developer to monitor wich features are used or not in order to improve « BibliBooks ». The anonymous data is not used for any commercial purpose and remains at the executive use of the developer.
